1) download main.cpp, gameBoard.cpp, and gameBoard.h
2) open replit, click the "+" on the top right corner
3) under the template options, click on "c++"
4) click "+ Create Repl "
5) under the "files" column on the left of the screen, click the 3 dots on the top right of the "files" column
6) find "upload file" and upload main.cpp, gameBoard.cpp, and gameBoard.h from your downloads. 
7) when you are ready, click the green "run" button on the top middle of the screen
